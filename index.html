Interactive Solar System

Planets: Mercury(M), Venus(V), Earth(E), Mars(R), Jupiter(J), Saturn(S), Uranus(U), Neptune(N)

Moons: Earth's Moon, Jupiter's 4 Galilean moons, Saturn's Titan

Features: Realistic textures, Saturn's rings, thousands of colored stars, persistent settings

Controls:

Arrow Keys: Move view | Q: Move forward | A: Move backward

Mouse: Look around | Scroll: Zoom in/out

P: Toggle orbital paths

0-9: Speed control (0=stop, 9=full speed)

Planet Focus: M, V, E, R, J, S, U, N

ESC: Release planet focus

Touch: Drag to look around

Pinch: Zoom in/out

Speed: Use keyboard 0-9 keys
